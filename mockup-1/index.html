Why Lyving

Everything sorted, so you can just live.

🛋️

All-Inclusive Living

Your monthly rent covers interior design, furniture, 100 Mbps Wi-Fi, and every utility bill, plus a monthly air-conditioning credit. One payment, zero surprises.

🔒

Privacy & Exclusivity

100% private rooms for single occupants. Units are single-gender for comfort, with limited master rooms available for couples.

🛡️

Security & Peace

3-tier access-card security and clear house rules, including quiet hours after 11 PM, so every home stays calm and respectful.

🧽

Professional Housekeeping

Our in-house team cleans shared areas, kitchens, and bathrooms every two weeks. Maintain a high standard without lifting a finger.

💬

Dedicated Support

A dedicated Property Manager is one message away for repairs, maintenance, or anything else, any day of the week.

Smart Living App

Pay rent, monitor smart-meter usage, and log requests from one seamless app. Everything transparent, everything tracked.
